Project Title

InterLinking of River National Water Ways Model and Case Study

Project Objective

The main aim of the project is to study and propose the interlinking of rivers as a sustainable water resource management strategy, focusing on its potential to:

  • Reduce regional water scarcity.
  • Improve irrigation facilities.
  • Enhance navigation through National Waterways.
  • Generate hydroelectric power.
  • Minimize flood and drought risks.

Project Description

This project examines the ...

concept of interlinking rivers in India and its integration with National Waterways to improve water transportation, irrigation, and regional development.

1.Model Development

  • A scaled physical model was created to demonstrate how rivers can be interconnected.
  • Showed water diversion, canal alignment, and lock systems used in National Waterways.
  • Represented the use of dams, reservoirs, and canals to balance water flow between surplus and deficit basins.
